What We Use

Galvanized Square Steel

Strong and resistant to corrosion, this steel is frequently utilized for reinforcing, beams, and framing to provide structural support.

Eco-friendly Wood Veneers

With a minimal harmful impact on the environment, these slices of real wood are glued to a less expensive core material to create a lovely, natural look.

Expansion Screws

These are specialty fasteners, often used for mounting cabinets, shelves, or fixtures, that offer a firm grip in hollow walls or pre-drilled holes.
